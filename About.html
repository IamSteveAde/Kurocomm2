About Us

Who We Are

Kuro Communications Limited is a reputable Concepts Development, Projects Management and Strategic Innovative Consulting Company that strives to provide solutions that fit into organization's requirements. When it comes to Capacity Building, Seminars, Project Management, Public Relations, Events Management and Creative Branding, KCL is your best bet.

Our passion for excellence in solutions delivery makes us combine eclectic and diverse mix in commitment, professional knowledge, logistics expertise, intellectual capacity, creativity and unique staff capabilities in making sure that our solutions meet a global best practices standard. This being our core value acts as our driving force in striving to plan and execute our client's Projects to perfection, far beyond their imagination.

What We Do

Kuro Communications Ltd (KCL) provides critical thinking at the critical time. Clients retain us because we combine unparalleled expertise with innovative thought leadership to address critical challenges, in both event-driven and long-term scenarios.

Our unrivalled team of professionals delivers expertise born from experience. Clients rely on our undiluted critical thinking that evolves only from decades of hands-on experience at every level. Our proffered solutions are practical, immediate and sustainable.

Our team of experts bring in a diverse set of skills and industry experience and as such serve a broad range of international companies, MDAs and private sector firms.We in turn share our clients' aspirations and deliver true results.

Our Team

Our people are our most important asset.
